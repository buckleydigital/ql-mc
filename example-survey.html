FAQ

Common questions from Sydney business owners

How much does commercial solar cost in Sydney in 2026?
A commercial system for a typical Sydney SME (30kW–100kW) typically costs $25,000–$90,000 before rebates and tax incentives. After applying the federal STC rebate and the instant asset write-off, the effective net cost is substantially lower. Your matched installer will provide a detailed ROI projection including all applicable incentives specific to your business.
What is the 2026 rebate and why does it decrease?
The federal government's Small-scale Technology Certificate (STC) scheme provides an upfront discount on commercial solar systems under 100kW. The rebate value reduces each year as the scheme's deeming period shortens, and it phases out completely by 2030. Installing in 2026 means a larger rebate than 2027 — every year you wait costs your business more upfront.
Can my business claim the instant asset write-off on solar?
Eligible businesses with a turnover under $50 million may be able to deduct the full cost of a commercial solar system from taxable income in the year of installation under the instant asset write-off provisions. This can represent a 25–30% effective tax saving on the system cost. We strongly recommend confirming with your accountant before proceeding — it's one of the most compelling commercial solar incentives available.
Does my business need to own the building to go solar?
Ideally yes — building ownership makes the process straightforward. If you lease your premises, it's still possible but requires landlord approval and a lease term that supports a reasonable payback period. Our commercial specialists can advise on lease situations. If you're unsure, it's worth completing the assessment — it costs nothing and the answer is often more positive than business owners expect.
How long does a commercial solar installation take?
Most SME commercial systems (up to 100kW) are installed within 4–8 weeks of approval, with typical installation taking 1–3 days with minimal disruption to your operations. Larger systems (100kW+) may require additional engineering sign-off and grid connection applications, extending the timeline to 8–16 weeks. Your specialist will give you a clear timeline upfront.
Is this service really free? What's the catch?
Genuinely free. We may earn a referral fee from the installer only if you choose to proceed — and it never affects your quoted price. The matching service and assessment are completely free with zero obligation, ever. If you don't proceed, that's fine — no follow-ups, no pressure.
