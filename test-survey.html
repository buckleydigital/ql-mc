FAQ

Common questions about this test survey

What is this survey for?
This is an internal test survey used to verify the survey template's functionality — including step navigation, postcode lookup, form validation, and lead submission — before deploying to a live client campaign.
Will submitting this form create a real lead?
Yes — submissions go to the real submit-lead edge function and will create a lead record in the database tagged with the configured niche and source. Use test data when running through the flow, and delete any test leads afterwards.
How do I test the disqualification path?
On Step 1, select "No, I'm Not the Decision Maker". The form cards should hide and the disqualification message should appear. Refreshing the page resets the survey to the beginning.
How do I test postcode coverage states?
Enter any 4-digit Australian postcode after selecting a bill range. The chip will show "Checking coverage…" while the lookup runs, then update to reflect whether a buyer is matched for that postcode and niche.
How do I test the duplicate lead error?
Submit the same phone or email address twice within 7 days. The second submission should return a duplicate status and display the error banner with the existing lead ID.
